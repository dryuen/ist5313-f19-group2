Reward Process

When training your dog, it is important to know that dogs will respond better to positive, rather than negative attention. Your dog will be more likely to perform the trick you requested when they will have a positive consequence as a result. Do not punish your dog when they do not perform the task you requested, but instead ignore the undesired behavior, and only reward them with it is performed correctly. By rewarding your dog when they perform the trick correctly, you will increase the bond between you and your dog, because they will trust you by providing this positive reinforcement. Throughout the process, you want the dog to feel like they are doing something fun, and keep your demeanor calm.
