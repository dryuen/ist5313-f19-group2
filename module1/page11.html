Maintaining

The best way to make sure your dog continues to practice the behaviors you want is to practice with them regularly. Do not expect your dog to recall tricks that you have not practiced with them recently. Once you have these routines set, continue to follow them daily. For example, before feeding your dog at meal times, have them use the "sit" command, since that will happen daily. Consistency is key.
