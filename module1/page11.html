Reinforcement Process

Dogs like routine. Tricks cannot be performed only when the owner feels like it, or remembers, otherwise the dog may forget the trick. When learning, the dog owner should practice the trick daily, or if needed, every other day. Practicing only once a week will not ingrain the skill within the dog. You should practice daily with your dog, in increments of 15 to 30 minutes. Twice per day will be plenty to ensure you are not getting too much practice to overwhelm your dog, but getting enough practice to ensure they learn the skill.
