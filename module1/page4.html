Introduction to Sit

Learning how to sit is a fundamental skill that many dogs learn early, quickly, and relatively easily. Make sure that as you learn this trick with your dog, that you are following the reward process outlined earlier in the module. This is an important foundational skill because you will normally begin other tricks or skills by first having your dog sit.
