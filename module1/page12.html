Simple Tricks Review

It is important to train your dog as soon as you bring them home to set routines and bond with them. Remember to always reward positive behavior with a training treat, for example, and to ignore negative behaviors. Go in order of the tricks as they appeared in the module, as they build on one another. When your dog isn't learning as quickly as you would like, do not get frustrated, your dog will sense that from you. You must remain patient and calm, and follow the positive reinforcement model. They will always respond best to this method.
