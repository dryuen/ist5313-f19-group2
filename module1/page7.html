Roll Over

Begin by using the "sit" command
Kneel beside your dog holding a training treat to the side of the dog's head near their nose.
Move your hand from the dog's nose toward their shoulder luring them to roll flat on their side.
You will want to perform the trick a few times, rewarding with a treat each time they follow the treat and lies flat on their side with their head on the floor.
Once they are lying flat, continue the movement of your hand from his shoulder to his backbone, still while holding a treat. You are using the same kind of movement, this time to roll onto their back.
Continue moving the treat hand so they rolls onto the other side.
When they are consistently following the treat all the way around in a roll over,add the verbal cue "Roll Over"
Repeat the trick multiple times to ensure your dog remembers it
