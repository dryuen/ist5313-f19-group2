Shake

Teaching your dog to shake is another simple task that your dog will learn with ease as long as you follow the same training steps from learning to sit. You may run into a common issue with this trick, as some dogs do not like their paws touched. In this case, you will need to build trust with your dog that nothing negative will happen as you touch their paw. Reward with a treat each time they let you touch their paw without pulling away. Continue to use the positive reinforcement method to assure your dog that they will be safe throughout this trick.
