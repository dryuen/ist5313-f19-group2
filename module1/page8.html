Simple Tricks

Teaching your dog to shake is another simple task that your dog will learn with ease as long as you follow the same training steps from learning to sit. Don't get discouraged if your dog does not learn it immediately, as some dogs do not like their paws touched. If this is the case, you will need to use the positive reinforcement method to ensure to your dog that they will be safe throughout this trick.
