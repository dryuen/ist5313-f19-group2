Listen to the introduction audio
Dogs are referred to as man's best friend. When a dog is well-trained, they will bond with their owner, listen to commands, and are less likely to eat their owner's dinner! The goal of this module is to assist the owner in training their own dog, instead of having to pay a pricey dog training school. There are many benefits of training your own dog, such as the bonding experience the dog and owner will go through. To complete this course, you do not have to have any knowledge or experience with dog training. It is ideal for first time owners, those in need of a refresher, to either puppies or grown dogs. In this module, you will learn simple tricks to perform with your dog: Sit, Shake, Roll-Over, Positive Reinforcement, and a system of giving rewards.
