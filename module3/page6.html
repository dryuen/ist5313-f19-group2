Potty Training

Having an adult dog is just as rewarding as having a puppy. When it comes to house training older dogs, they are as eager to bond just as puppies are. A perk of house training an older dog would be that it is an easier process for them. Although adult dogs have the ability to "hold it" for several hours compared to a puppy, you should still take your adult dog out to provide several opportunities to learn.
