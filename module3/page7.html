Potty Training

Just like setting routines, this should be included in their day to day schedule. If their morning routine includes a wake up, potty break, then feeding, make sure you are consistent in keeping that schedule. Having a set feeding time will help your dog stick to a bathroom schedule, since they are eating at the same time every day. The easiest would be to choose a time in the morning and evening that you are home as a part of your routine to feed them. For example, feed your dog before you leave for work/school, and when you come home. This will decrease anxiety levels for your dogs because they will know when to expect a meal.
