Potty Training

When house/potty training your dog, it is important to know that dogs will respond better to positive attention. It is important to remain patient and consistent to create a smooth process. After your dog uses the potty you can positively reinforce the behavior with kind words. Going a step beyond verbal praise, you can also reinforce good potty behavior with a reasonable treat that your dog enjoys. Showing physical affection to your dog, such as a loving touch, or a light pat, would also provide as a form of positive reinforcement. Once your dog has completed a practiced skill like using the bathroom outdoors, it is important to reward them immediately afterwards with praise or small treats. This step is important because it will help your dog know what is expected of them.
