Potty Training

Puppy pads are an alternative to going outside if you have very young puppies who may have not had all their shots and cannot go outside, senior dogs who have trouble going outside on walks, and a dog at any age who is still training to go outside to use the bathroom. Make sure if you are using puppy pads that you are being consistent with the location so the dog doesn't have to search for them, and won't go on the floor in their place. If you are using them to help your dog train to go outside, place them as close to the door you will be using with your dog to leave to go out to associate with it.
