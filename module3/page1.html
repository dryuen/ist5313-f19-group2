Potty Training

Listen to the introduction audio
Potty training is an important skill that some owners find difficult, or scary to attempt, but just like the other skills you have learned in this course so far, it will be just as easy with practice and consistency. It is important to teach your dog to go to the bathroom where you intend them to early, to enforce these habits as soon as you bring them into their home. You want to make sure they learn these imporant things at the begining with you, instead of retraining them with behaviors they have taught themselves.
