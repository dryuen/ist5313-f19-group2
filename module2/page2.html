How to Walk on a Leash

It is important to be prepared before you begin leash training, because the trip out of the home can have many distractions. Preparing for the walk will prevent your dog from being overstimulated, and ensure they are ready to train, since there are a number of things your dog will need to focus on while they are on the walk, such as making sure they are walking next to you, and processing the new people and smells they encounter. Your dog will want to wander to these smells and people naturally, and they will just need training to prevent these behaviors. Remember to stay patient and calm while training your dog to walk with you on the leash, and be ready with training treats.
