Leash Training

You may decide you want to go off leash with your dog. Keep in mind that this takes months of training and cannot be done within days or weeks of the initial leash training. Consider your dog's walk habits, and attempt to take them off leash for walks in enclosed spaces such as back yards and dog parks before attempting to take them out. It is not impossible to do so, but instead, make sure you know your dog, and your own confidence with commands and training first.
