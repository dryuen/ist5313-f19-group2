What to do When your Dog Pulls

One strategy that might be helpful is to put your dog on a chest harness, either while they are learning, or permanently if you find that your dog responds better to the chest harness. The techniques for training are still the same. One reason dogs pulls is that when they pull, and you pull back, it is reinforcing their pulling behavior. When the dog pulls, stop, and either reverse directions, or say "let's go."
