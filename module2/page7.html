Leash Training

To ensure your dog continues to behave the way you want on each walk, you must practice these skils on every walk you go on, even if it means an emergency three in the morning walk to visit the bathroom. Just like all of the tricks and commands you have used so far, practice builds the skill and relationship between you and your dog. Over time, you will not have as much need to correct behaviors.
