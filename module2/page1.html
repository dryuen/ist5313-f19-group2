Leash Training

Listen to the introduction audio
After learning these simple tricks to complete with your dog inside the home, it will become necessary to take your dog outside for walks, socialization, and bathroom breaks. It is important to teach your dog how to behave on a leash early, because they will see how you perform on the leash with them as training. How you behave with them is what they will expect on future walks. Since you are the owner, they will take cues from you.
