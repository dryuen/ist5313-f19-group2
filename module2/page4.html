Where to Practice

Finding a place to practice is not as difficult as it seems. If you have a fenced yard, that will be best, because of the dog does escape the leash, they will still be safe in the yard. Other good places are dog parks, dog friendly parks, areas that are not well populated, and free of distractions. Try to stay in or around your neighborhood.
